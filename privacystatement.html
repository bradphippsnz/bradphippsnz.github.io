HAUKAI RESTAURANT PRIVACY STATEMENT

We collect personal information from you, including your name, contact information and any interactions with us.

Your personal information is collected in order to improve our customer service and enhance the user experience.

Providing some information is entirely optional. However if you choose not to enter your personal information, we'll be unable to share with you any future offerings and meal discounts with you.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.

If you'd like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at haukai@gmail.com, phone us at (09) 401-4019, or you can mail us at 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Bay of Islands.
