Tēnā koutou katoa

Kia ora and welcome to the Haukai Restaurant.

Nestled in the heart of the beautiful Bay of Islands, Haukai is an unmissable dining experience.

Our goal is to fill your puku with the most delicious kai, made with aroha from the best local ingredients.

Sit indoors surrounded by fine artwork by local artists or relax outdoors and watch the sun go down as we prepare delicious Māori and Pākehā kai with a modern spin for you.

Bring your whole whānau down and experience Haukai Restaurant - he rawe!

Open Wednesday to Sunday from 5pm
